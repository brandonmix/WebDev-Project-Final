Top 10 NBA Players of All Time

The NBA has been around for 75 years and it's never been better. It's something I love to follow, so here are my top 10 players of all time:

  1. Michael Jordan
    • MJ has been to the Finals 6 times and won all 6.
    • He only played 13 years in the league and made it to 3rd on the All-Time scoring list when he retired.
    • He averaged over 30 ppg in his career.
    • He has the biggest shoe brand today and was also the first NBA player to own an NBA team.
  2. Lebron James
  3. Kareem Abdul-Jabbar
  4. Magic Johnson
  5. Stephen Curry
  6. Larry Bird
  7. Shaquille O'Neal
  8. Kobe Bryant
  9. Tim Duncan
  10. Kevin Durant
